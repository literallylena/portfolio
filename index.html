My Name is
Lena Bär.

I completed my training as a Media Designer in 2022. Since then, I've had the oppor­tunity to work in various crea­tive roles, begin­ning in the De­sign and Marke­ting depart­ment within the educa­tion sector. Follow­ing that, I took on the role of Art Direc­tor for a jewel­ry brand, where I refined my skills in bran­ding and visual story­telling.

Based in Berlin, I also create graphics, logos, web­sites, and print products for events and small labels, bring­ing creative visions to life with a focus on detail and aesthet­ics.

art direction

Product photo­graphy I did for JETTE SP24.

As Art Director for the jewelry brand JETTE, I was respon­sible for crea­ting and execu­ting visual con­tent that cap­tured the brand's iden­tity and the ele­gance of its collec­tions. From devel­oping creative con­cepts and planning photo­shoots to oversee­ing produc­tion on set and han­dling post-production (image and video edit­ing), I man­aged the entire crea­tive process. The final content was fea­tured on JETTE's social media chan­nels and website, enhanc­ing the brand's visual presence and engag­ing its target audi­ence emotion­ally. The show­cased works offer a glimpse into the photo­shoots I con­tributed to – from strik­­ing imagery to contem­porary video content.
